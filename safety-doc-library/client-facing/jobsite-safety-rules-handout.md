## [COMPANY NAME] — Jobsite Safety Rules

1. Wear required PPE: [hard hat, eye protection, hi-vis, safety-toe boots, gloves].
2. Inspect tools, ladders, and equipment before use; don't use damaged equipment.
3. Use fall protection when working at height.
4. Keep the site clean; keep walkways and exits clear.
5. Follow lockout/tagout before servicing equipment.
6. Read labels/SDSs before using chemicals.
7. Report all hazards, near misses, and injuries immediately.
8. You have stop-work authority — use it if something is unsafe.
9. No horseplay; no phone use during safety-sensitive tasks.
10. Know the emergency procedures and muster point.
