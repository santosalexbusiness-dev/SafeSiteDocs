## Template Body
Template for answering a client's safety questionnaire. Customize answers to your actual program — be accurate, not aspirational.

**Company:** [COMPANY NAME]   **Prepared by:** [NAME]   **Date:** [DATE]

Common questions and where to source the answer:
1. Do you have a written safety program? → Safety Program Summary
2. How do you train workers? → Training matrix, orientation, toolbox talks
3. How do you manage hazards? → JHAs and pre-task planning
4. How do you handle incidents? → Incident reporting/investigation process
5. What is your injury/illness history? → Your records (if tracked)
6. What PPE do you require? → PPE policy and hazard assessment
7. How do you manage subcontractors? → Subcontractor agreement/prequalification
8. Insurance coverage? → Certificates of insurance

**Note:** answer only what you can support with documentation. Do not claim certifications or outcomes you cannot verify.
