## Section 1 — Welcome & Safety Commitment
At **[COMPANY NAME]**, safety is part of every job. We expect you to work safely, look out for your coworkers, and speak up about hazards. You will never be disciplined for reporting a hazard or for stopping work you reasonably believe is unsafe.

## Section 2 — Your Safety Responsibilities
- Follow safe work practices and this company's safety rules.
- Use required PPE and inspect it before use.
- Inspect tools and equipment before use; don't use damaged equipment.
- Report all injuries, illnesses, near misses, and property damage to your supervisor right away.
- Ask when you're unsure. Use your **stop-work authority** when needed.

## Section 3 — Key Programs (overview)
