## How to Use This Template
Inspect each ladder **before use** and on a periodic schedule. Check every item below. If any item is marked **Fail**, remove the ladder from service immediately, tag it "DO NOT USE," and report it. Keep completed checklists on file. Train employees to perform this inspection.

## Inspection Items

| # | Item | Pass | Fail | N/A | Notes |

## Result
☐ **PASS** — Ladder is safe to use.
☐ **FAIL** — Remove from service immediately, tag "DO NOT USE," and report. (Use the *Defective Ladder Tag*.)

**Action taken:** [DESCRIBE]   **Reported to:** [NAME]   **Date removed from service:** [DATE]

**Reminder — safe setup (verify at use):** correct angle for extension ladders, secured top and bottom, extends above the landing where required, on stable level ground, three points of contact when climbing. *(See the Ladder Setup Guide.)*
